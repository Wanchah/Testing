Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with EduMorph?

Simply create an account, choose your age group and interests, and start exploring our AI-powered learning materials. You can upload documents, process YouTube videos, or use our AI chat for personalized learning.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF, DOCX, TXT, PPT, PPTX, and MD files. We also support YouTube video links for content processing and note generation.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All uploaded content is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties.

Can I use EduMorph offline?

Currently, EduMorph requires an internet connection for AI processing and content generation. However, you can download generated materials for offline study.

How accurate is the AI-generated content?

Our AI models are trained on high-quality educational content and provide accurate summaries, notes, and study materials. However, we recommend reviewing generated content for your specific learning needs.

What age groups is EduMorph designed for?

EduMorph is designed for learners of all ages: Children (5-12), Teens (13-17), Young Adults (18-25), and Adults (26+). Content is automatically adapted to be age-appropriate.
